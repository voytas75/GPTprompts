# 156. Master Educator: Mental Model Selection

```text

Then, evaluate each model’s strengths, weaknesses, and applicability based on core principles. Finally, select and explain the most accurate and useful mental model in detail, and provide a concise justification for why it’s best.

Constraints:
- Keep your internal deliberation private; share only clear conclusions and brief justifications.
- Use headings: Models, Evaluation, Best Model, Summary.
```
